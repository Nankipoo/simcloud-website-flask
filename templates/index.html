HOW IT WORKS

A simple, free online registration will give you a secure login to your own SIMcloud wallet and management portal. Top up your wallet in real time by making a deposit into the SIMcloud bank account with your registered cell number as the unique reference number. A verification process may follow your very first payment. After verification, all subsequent payments will be applied instantly, 24/7. Now capture and organize all the mobile numbers in your fleet into convenient operational lists. To recharge, select the list or individual numbers, choose the amount and hit GO… your entire list will be recharged while you make the coffee. Edit and manage your lists and track spending per mobile number. Monthly Tax Invoices and reports are accessible online.
